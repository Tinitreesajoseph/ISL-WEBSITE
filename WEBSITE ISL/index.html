KERALA BLASTERS FC

TOP TIER IN FOOTBALL HISTORY

Kerala Blasters Football Club, commonly referred to as The Blasters, is an Indian professional football club based in Kochi, Kerala, that competes in the Indian Super League (ISL). The club was established in May 2014 during the inaugural season of the Indian Super League.

HOME STADIUM

Our Home, Jawaharlal Nehru International Stadium, popularly known as the Kaloor Stadium situated in Kochi, Kerala is one of India’s largest and most modern venues.

Capacity

Since the 2017 renovation, the stadium has a seating capacity of 80,234.

Unique Shape

Triple-tiered stands, sloping and contemporary roof structure, modern turf.

Lighting

The lighting towers of 2kW floodlights are one of the most innovative aspects.
